Task 3 - Analytics and Commercial Application

Prepared a category review presentation for Quantium's retail client analysing chip purchasing behaviour and trial store performance.

Key Findings:
- Top 3 customer segments: Budget Older Families, Mainstream Young Singles/Couples and Mainstream Retirees
- Kettle Chips was the most popular brand, 175g the top pack size
- Mainstream customers are the largest chip buyers across all life stages
- Older and Young Families buy the most chips regardless of affluence

Trial Store Results:
- Store 77: 32.5% sales uplift
- Store 86: 13.2% sales uplift
- Store 88: 12.2% sales uplift
- Sales increase was driven by more purchasing customers, not higher spend per customer
